About Patty

Patty is a personal finance application focused on practical day-to-day money management. Our goal is to provide users with clear visibility into spending, subscriptions, and savings progress.

Our Mission

We believe financial tools should be understandable, efficient, and respectful of user trust. Patty is built to reduce complexity and help users make informed financial decisions.

What We Value

  • Clarity in product design and communication
  • Security and privacy by default
  • Reliable features that solve real user needs
