Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Patty ("we," "our," or "us") handles user information in connection with the Patty mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Data Collection and Use

At this time, Patty does not collect personal data from users for storage, sale, or profiling purposes. We do not sell user data and do not disclose user information to unrelated third parties for advertising purposes.

2. Future Features

Patty may introduce future features requiring limited user identity information, such as a shared wallet capability. As of the effective date of this policy, shared wallets are not implemented.

If and when shared wallets become available, we expect to use Google services (including Google Sign-In) to authenticate users. In that scenario, Patty would only process the minimum information necessary to identify participating users and enable wallet sharing with the intended recipients.

3. Third-Party Services

Where Google services are used, Google may process information under its own terms and privacy policies. Patty does not sell or share user information with other third parties beyond what is required to operate the Service securely and as intended.

4. Data Sharing Commitment

We do not share user information with third parties for marketing, brokerage, or unrelated commercial purposes.

5. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect operational, legal, or product changes. Any updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

6. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us through the official Patty support channel.
